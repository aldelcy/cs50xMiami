After CS50x

The purpose of this section is to share some resources and guidance as to what your options are after taking CS50x. Now is the time to figure out what direction you would like to take in Computer Science. CS50x can give you the foundation needed to follow a career in many different fields from Web Development to Data Science to even Cyber Security. However, a foundation can only take you so far and we would like to share some of the resources that can help you accomplish that, hopefully making your journey just a little bit easier. Below are some of the tracks we feel are perfect routes to take after CS50x should you decide that is what excites you.

Web Development Track

  • The Complete 2020 Web Development Bootcamp on Udemy.
  • The Advanced Web Developer Bootcamp
  • FreeCodeCamp

Mobile Development Track

  • The Complete React Native on Udemy.
  • IOS 13 & Swift 5 on Udemy.

Data Science Track

  • The Modern Python Bootcamp on Udemy.
  • Data Science A-Z on Udemy.
